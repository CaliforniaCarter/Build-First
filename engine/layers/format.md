# Format layer (universal, light-touch)

A light structural pass after the voice draft. It never rewrites the words — it only
checks the shape. The persona's never-do list always wins over anything here.

## Rules
- Hook in the first line (~140 characters). Make them stop. Never "excited to announce."
- One idea per post. Short lines. Use white space. Show the moment, don't summarize it.
- Close with a real question or the proof. No engagement bait.
- Length: aim ~600–1,500 characters. Restraint wins; shorter is fine if it flows.
- Over-formatting (bold everything, an emoji per line) reads as AI. Don't.
- Specific beats generic. Saves beat likes.

## Examples
- Good hook: "I deleted 200 lines of my AI agent yesterday. It got better."
- Bad hook: "Excited to share some thoughts on AI agents!"
- Good body: "The bug wasn't in the code. It was my prompt. One word, 'thorough,' quadrupled the slop."
- Bad body: "There are many important considerations when building AI agents."
