# Audience layer — Tenex

Who you're writing for tilts topic and proof, not your voice. For now the audience is
the one the Tenex team writes for (operators and execs going from AI-absent to
AI-native), with tone modeled on Alex Lieberman, Arman Hezarkhani, and Brett Franklin.

Goal of this run (from intake): leave them inspired and curious about how Carter could
add value — without selling.

## Levers
- Open on a real moment, not a thesis (Alex).
- Show the actual mechanism, and attach proof (Arman).
- Land one real number when you have one.
- Specific and ownable — only you could have written it. No generic trend takes.
- Show you shipped it and used it yourself (customer zero).

## Examples
- Good: "Built it for myself first. Here it is on my real work, and it cut my draft time from 40 minutes to 4."
- Bad: "AI can analyze your writing style and save you tons of time!"
